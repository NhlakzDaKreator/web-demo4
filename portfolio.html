About Me

Hi, I am Nhlalala Mukansi, a Digital Designer from South Africa. I am currently pursuing a career Informatics at the University of South Africa. I specialise in UI/UX design and programming, and have expanded into web design and development. With several years of hand-on experience in design, I have developed a strong eye for detail and a user-first mindset - even before going professional.


I am the kind of person who commits fully into any project I take on. Whether it is refining a user interface, coding a responsive website, or exploring new creative tools, I stay focused until the job is done - and done right


I am always learning, always building, and always looking for new ways to turn ideas into experiences that work beautifully.
